Pain Points

The biggest pain points in web scraping

Bot detection & IP bans

Automated traffic gets flagged as bots within seconds. Target sites blacklist entire IP ranges, terminate sessions mid-crawl, and silently serve junk data instead of returning errors.

Geo restrictions

Content, pricing, and search results vary by region. Scraping from a single location gives you an incomplete — and often inaccurate — view of the market.

CAPTCHAs & rate limits

WAFs and anti-bot systems throttle your pipeline. CAPTCHA challenges and per-IP rate limits burn through retries and drive up infrastructure costs.

High maintenance cost

Building and managing proxy rotation, retry logic, and IP pools in-house drains engineering time — time better spent on the data your business actually needs.
