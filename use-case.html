Common Challenges

Why scrapers fail — and how to fix it

Bot detection & IP bans

Automated traffic gets fingerprinted within seconds. Sites block entire IP ranges, kill sessions mid-crawl, and silently return junk data instead of errors.

Geo restrictions

Prices, content, and search results differ by region. Scraping from a single location gives you an incomplete — and often wrong — picture of the market.

CAPTCHAs & rate limits

WAFs and anti-bot layers slow pipelines to a crawl. CAPTCHA challenges and per-IP rate limits waste retries and inflate infrastructure cost.

High maintenance cost

Managing proxy pools, rotation logic, and retry queues in-house drains engineering time — time better spent on the data your business actually needs.
